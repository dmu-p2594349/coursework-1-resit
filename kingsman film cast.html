ABOUT

Colin Firth

Colin Firth was born in Grayshott, Hampshire England in 1960. His first real big break he is known for is Bridget Jones Diary and then went on to play roles in Mumma mia and a single man. The kingsman role seemed very different from his other parts due too him playing more of a serious character. He plays Harry.galahad in this movie one of the main heads of the secret service.

Taron Egerton

Taron Egerton was born in Berkenhead, cheshire England in 1989. Tarons first big break which people know him for is Kingsmen where he plays the character Eggy. soon after the first Kingsman he then played Eddie in 'Eddie The Eagle' then the sequal to kingsman in 2017 and played Elton John in 'Rocketman'.
